Title: Natalie Dang

----

Position: CEO

----

Text: Natalie (Dang Binlan) is the CEO and Director of Super Organic Dairy and Director of Maui Milk. She currently spends her time between Auckland and Shanghai. For over the past 5 years Natalie has positioned NZ Sheep Dairy products as a premium product in the Chinese market. Initially with Shanghai Blue River, now she runs Shanghai Maui Food Group, which is the Chinese market distributor for Maui Milk.<br>Prior to her involvement in the sheep dairy industry, Natalie spent a number of years introducing high-end clothing brands into the Chinese market.
